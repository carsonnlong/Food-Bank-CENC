Call to Action

Determining the main task for a site visitor helps decide what should be on the home page.

*This is my first draft of my style and CTA

For my call to action, I want to help streamline and simplify the process that users will face when visiting the Food Bank of Central and Eastern North Carolina website. The groups of people that visit the site can be broken down into two major categories: those who need the help and resources of the organization, and those that wish to get involved and help those in need. To help sort the viewer into one of these categories, my call to action will be a banner on the main home page that shows two images, each containing a button. The buttons read, “GET HELP” and “GIVE HELP”. This will help the visitor know exactly where to go to find information they need, and it helps avoid the overload of information that is on their home page by sorting it into two categories.
