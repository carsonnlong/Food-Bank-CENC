Design Challenge

Completing this exercise helped me brainstorm ways this redesign can help the organization.

1. Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How does the website for Food Bank of CENC help the organization end hunger?

2. Now, state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

To increase donations to the Food Bank and enable people to get help/give help easily.

3. What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Simplified information/design, easier website navigation, more consistent brand standards, improved aesthetics to increase credibility, mobile site

4. Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

The organization operates only in NC. FBCENC is part of Feeding America, which means that their branding/visuals need to work together. They utilize social media often, which means that the site visuals need to be flexible.

5. Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can the FBCENC website use simplicity and consistency to encourage donations to and participation in the organization?
