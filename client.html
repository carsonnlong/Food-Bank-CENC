Client Choice & Why

Food Bank of Central and Eastern North Carolina

The Food Bank CENC provides food and resources to those in need across North Carolina and has a wide range of opportunities for the community to get involved by donating food, money, or time.

The Food Bank CENC site is very complicated and hard to navigate. Overall there is too much information on any given page for a user to be able to digest it. There is also no clear sense of hierarchy or legibility in their structure of information on the pages. Aside from functional issues, the website is also not designed well aesthetically.
